### Search Merchant Insights
```text
/merchant-insights/merchants?
  merchantName={{merchantName}}&
  streetAddress={{streetAddress}}&
  city={{city}}&
  stateCode={{stateCode}}&
  postalCode={{postalCode}}
```

### Get Merchant Insights by MerchantId
```text
/merchant-insights/merchants/{{merchantId}}
```
